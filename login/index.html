Scoring Methodology
How AXL scores and ranks each lead from 0–100
Signal Freshness30%
How recent is the frustration signal? Signals within 30 days score highest. A signal from 6+ months ago drops significantly regardless of severity, as intent may have cooled or the prospect may have already moved to a competitor.
Decision-Maker Access25%
Is the lead a CEO, founder, or budget holder with direct purchasing authority? Direct access to the decision-maker dramatically increases conversion probability compared to mid-level contacts who require internal approvals.
Product Fit25%
How many NetContenta features directly address the lead's documented pain points? A 4-feature match scores higher than a 1-feature match. Fit is assessed against AI Voice, AI Chat, Ticketing, and Admin Dashboard.
Urgency / Intent20%
Is the lead actively seeking alternatives (active cancellation, public ask), or passively dissatisfied (mild complaints)? Active signals score 2–3x higher than passive dissatisfaction with no stated action.
Score Interpretation
What each range means for outreach priority
85–100
Hot — Contact Immediately
In active search or cancellation. Every day of delay risks losing to a competitor. Personalized outreach within 48 hours is critical.
65–84
Warm — Engage This Week
Showing clear intent signals but not yet in crisis. Has a window to capture attention before they make a decision. Schedule outreach within 5–7 days.
40–64
Lukewarm — Nurture
Some fit and mild dissatisfaction but no active buying signals yet. Add to nurture sequence and revisit in 30 days or when a new signal is detected.
